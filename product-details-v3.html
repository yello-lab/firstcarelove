  • How to Use
Rejuvenate and refresh your skin with our Rosewater Hydrating Mist. Infused with the essence of roses, this luxurious mist provides a burst of hydration, soothing and revitalizing your skin. The delicate fragrance uplifts your senses, while the antioxidant-rich formula helps to tone and balance your complexion. Spritz it on for an instant pick-me-up throughout the day, leaving your skin feeling hydrated, radiant, and revitalized.
  • Synthetic Silk Fibers: Mimic the look and feel of natural lashes, providing a soft and lightweight texture.
  • Latex-Free Adhesive: Gentle and safe formula to ensure secure and long-lasting hold.
  • Flexible Band: Designed for comfort and easy application, offering a seamless blend with your natural lashes.
  • Handcrafted Quality: Each lash is carefully handcrafted to achieve a flawless and natural appearance.
  • Cleanse your face and pat dry.
  • Apply an even layer of the Charcoal Detoxifying Mask to your face, avoiding the eye and lip areas.
  • Leave the mask on for 10-15 minutes or until it's completely dry.
  • Gently rinse off with warm water, massaging in circular motions for added exfoliation.
  • Pat your skin dry and follow with your favorite moisturizer.
  • For optimal results, use the mask 2-3 times per week.
